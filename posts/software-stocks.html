Software Stocks Fall

February 12, 2026

Last week, software stocks gave up all their gains above the broader market since 2017. When I entered the investment industry in mid-2017, software stocks were almost universally known as "good businesses." But investors perceive artificial intelligence applications will undermine those business models. Maybe Salesforce and Adobe won't disappear but are individuals and companies going to pay what they used to pay? And will they agree to big price increases each year? Suddenly, those EV/Sales ratios that were once evidence of business quality are albatrosses on the share price as analysts increasingly discount future earnings.
